Akan Names

Akan people originate from ancient Ghana. The akan community frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born.Below is a calculator that can show you, wich day of the week you have been named after.

This web application takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name.
